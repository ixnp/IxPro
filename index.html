Stuff about me

You are only limited by your imagination. This is a happy place, little squirrels live here and play. Everyone wants to enjoy the good parts - but you have to build the framework first. Of course he's a happy little stone, cause we don't have any other kind. Let's give him a friend too. Everybody needs a friend. Isn't it fantastic that you can change your mind and create all these happy things? God gave you this gift of imagination. Use it. Use what you see, don't plan it. Let's make a happy little mountain now. Each highlight must have it's own private shadow. This is truly an almighty mountain. We tell people sometimes: we're like drug dealers, come into town and get everybody absolutely addicted to painting. It doesn't take much to get you addicted.
